About Me

Education and Tech Skills Background

Anthony E. Akel is a highly motivated Computer Engineering graduate who received his bachelors from the University of Michigan - Dearborn in December 2024. With a strong foundation in software development, Anthony has honed his skills through both academic and practical experiences, making him a valuable candidate for engineering and tech companies.

His work experience includes internships at MAHLE in Troy, MI, and Robert Bosch LLC in Novi, MI, where he gained hands-on experience in test bench engineering and strategy consulting, respectively. At MAHLE, he assisted with simulations and gathered measurements on various test benches, and contributed to the development of a closed-loop pressure regulator using Arduino. At Bosch, he supported data quality enhancement and automation initiatives, significantly improving processing efficiency.

Anthony's personal projects further showcase his technical versatility, with notable projects including a Python-based personal finance tracker, a sentiment analysis program, and a secure text analyzer utilizing Google Cloud technologies. His educational background is complemented by proficiency in a range of programming languages including C, C++, Python, JavaScript, MATLAB, HTML, SQL and VBA. He has also developed expertise in frameworks such as Node.js, PyTorch, Qt, Docker, and Kubernetes, which enable him to contribute effectively to innovative software development projects. His experience with microcontrollers like Jetson Nano, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and STM32 series, combined with his knowledge of multiple operating systems, positions him as a well-rounded candidate ready to tackle complex engineering challenges.

Anthony possesses a diverse skill set that includes software development, data analysis, API design, and cloud computing. His ability to work across various platforms and technologies, coupled with his proficiency in English, Arabic, and French, makes him an appealing prospect for tech companies looking for a versatile and detail-oriented software engineer
